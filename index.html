Meet Robert

Robert Ross Pe­ter­son earned his stripes at IBM where as a highly acclaimed hands-on technologist, he developed cutting edge applications at IBM and for top clients including Royal Bank of Scotland, Morgan Stanley, Macy's, and Express Scripts.
